About Us

Orinoco Alliance is a leading firm specializing in energy sectors, featuring a team of professionals with deep expertise in Hydrocarbons, Power, and Industrial Infrastructure both in Venezuela and globally.

We combine local insight with international standards to deliver solutions that drive Value Creation and Sustainable Development, enhancing the reliability of your investment portfolios.

Mission

To provide comprehensive energy solutions with operational excellence, fostering local development and complying with the highest ethical standards.

Vision

To be the undisputed benchmark in industrial and energy services in the region, recognized for our integrity and execution capacity.

Our Services

Strategic solutions designed to optimize every stage of the energy value chain.

Bidding Advisory

We provide strategic support for public and private bidding processes, including business meeting representation and the comprehensive preparation of technical plans and competitive economic proposals.

Asset Management

We apply ISO 55000 principles to optimize asset performance, aligning our services with your Strategic Asset Management Plan (SAMP) to ensure sustainable value creation.

Technical Consulting

We offer specialized technical and financial advisory services for energy and hydrocarbons projects, supported by a multidisciplinary team tailored to the needs of your investment portfolios.

Strategic Alliances

We facilitate strategic partnerships and networking initiatives that enhance the development of energy projects and strengthen the performance of your operations in the region.

Subcontracting Services

We coordinate and supervise subcontracted services with technical and financial rigor, conducting audits, contractual assessments, and legal analyses to ensure compliance, efficiency, and transparency.

Supply Chain Contracts

We design efficient supply chain and logistics solutions that enhance operational reliability, optimize costs, and ensure continuity across your activities in the Venezuelan energy market.

Corporate Policies

Anti-Corruption

We maintain a zero‑tolerance approach to any form of corruption, bribery, or improper conduct. Our business decisions are made ethically, transparently, and in full compliance with applicable laws and international standards. We expect the same level of integrity from all partners, suppliers, and contractors.

Anti-Discrimination

We promote an inclusive, respectful, and discrimination‑free work environment. We do not tolerate any practice based on race, gender, age, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, or any other protected characteristic. Diversity is a core value that strengthens our culture and enhances organizational performance.

Confidentiality

We rigorously protect the sensitive and strategic information entrusted to us by clients, partners, and collaborators. All information is handled with strict confidentiality and used solely for authorized purposes. We apply modern security practices and compliance standards to ensure data integrity and protection.

Ethics

Our actions are guided by principles of honesty, responsibility, and respect. We comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and international standards governing the energy sector, and we act in the legitimate interest of our clients and partners. Ethical conduct is a fundamental pillar of our corporate identity and decision‑making.
